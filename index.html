Thank-you for taking part in this listening test. It will take approximately 15 minutes. Results will be kept anonymous and confidential. Some web browsers are not supported.

Headphones and a quiet listening space are required.

Your information:







Instructions:

You will be presented with a number of seperate listening tests. Despite the word test, they are not a test of your ability but of human perception. It is therefore very important that you are honest about your answers.

Each condition has a play/pause button to start the audio in your headphones. It may take a few seconds for the audio to start. The audio may be music or speech. You will be presented with a slider that adjusts the amount of 'processing' on the audio, with furthest left 0% processing and furthest right 100% processing.

You must slowly move the slider from right to left until you perceive/notice a difference in the audio quality. You may move the slider to 100% processing and not hear a difference. You will also find the Master Volume slider, which simply controls the loudness of all conditions. Set this to a comfortable listening level at the beginning of the test.
